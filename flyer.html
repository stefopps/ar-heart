How to use

  1. Keep this flyer open on your PC (or print it).
  2. Scan LOCAL for live work on this machine, or GITHUB for the pushed build.
  3. Accept the camera prompt (and the cert warning on LOCAL).
  4. Point at the Hiro marker — status should say MARKER LOCKED and a green pad appears under the model.
  5. Desktop: lights / orientation auto-apply to AR and save into settings.json.
